Welcome!

Here you can view partially reconstructed statistics about historical farming contests on Hypixel SkyBlock. Currently has 16,000,000+ records from past contests. New data is added every few days.

Why? Hypixel lets you lookup every contest a player has participated in, but there's no way to list all players who participated in a specific contest. This website aims to make that possible.
